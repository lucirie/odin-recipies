Tiramisu Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 1 ¼ cups heavy cream
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese, at room temperature
  • ¼ cup strong brewed coffee, at room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

Whisk Egg Yolks and Sugar

Whisk egg yolks and sugar together in a medium saucepan until well blended.

Cook the Mixture

Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture comes to a boil.

Cool and Chill

Boil gently for 1 minute, then remove from the heat and allow to cool slightly. Cover tightly and chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour.

Beat the Cream

Beat cream and vanilla in a medium bowl with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.

Prepare the Filling

Remove the egg yolk mixture from the refrigerator; add mascarpone cheese and whisk until smooth.

Soak the Ladyfingers

Combine coffee and rum in a small bowl. Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise and drizzle with the coffee mixture. Arrange 1/2 of the soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a 7x11-inch dish.

Layer the Tiramisu

Spread 1/2 of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then spread 1/2 of the whipped cream over top. Repeat layers once more.

Dust with Cocoa Powder

Sprinkle cocoa powder over top.

Refrigerate

Cover and refrigerate until set, 4 to 6 hours.

Enjoy!

Serve chilled and enjoy your homemade tiramisu.
